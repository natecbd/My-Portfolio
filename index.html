    A JUMP START

    I started my developer career by studying at bloc.io, a program that pairs students with an expert in their field. I completed their Front-End Web Developement course in November of 2015. The site pictured is Bloc Jams, a simple music player with a focus on UI optimization and design.

    FIRST PROJECT

    During my time at Bloc I was approached by the Cascade Band Boosters to fix their dying website and fill their Webmaster position. They built the original website using Joomla, a powerful CMS but was not user friendly enough for their rotating staff of volunteer parents. After the site broke I rebuilt everything in Wordpress and taught them how to manage and update their content.
